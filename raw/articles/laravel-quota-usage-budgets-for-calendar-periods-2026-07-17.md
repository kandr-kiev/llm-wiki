                <p>Laravel Quota is a package for tracking and enforcing cumulative usage limits. For example, you could enforce limits like 50 PDF exports a month, 1,000 API queries a day, a pool of AI credits per billing cycle, and more.</p>
<p>It's aimed at a different problem than Laravel's <code>RateLimiter</code>: instead of throttling bursts of traffic in a sliding window, it counts consumption against a budget that resets on a calendar boundary, and it can persist that count in the database rather than only in the cache.</p>
<p>Here's what the package gives you:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Calendar-aligned periods</strong> — define <code>perMinute()</code>, <code>perHour()</code>, <code>perDay()</code>, <code>perWeek()</code>, <code>perMonth()</code>, <code>perYear()</code>, or <code>period($start, $end)</code> for a custom window</li>
<li><strong>A <code>HasQuotas</code> trait</strong> that puts quotas on any Eloquent model</li>
<li><strong>Route middleware</strong> (<code>quota:exports,50,month</code>) that only consumes on a successful response</li>
<li><strong>Cache or database backends</strong>, switchable per call</li>
<li><strong>Atomic consumption</strong> - lock consumption around the work it meters</li>
<li><strong>Enforce budgets</strong> - throw an HTTP 429 when the budget is spent</li>
<li>And more.</li>
</ul>

<p>The <code>consume()</code> method returns an immutable <code>QuotaInfo</code> DTO. If you'd rather fail hard than branch, the <code>enforce()</code> method throws an HTTP 429 when the budget is gone:</p>
<pre><code data-theme="github-light" data-lang="php" class='torchlight' style='background-color: #fff; --theme-selection-background: #e2e5e9;'><!-- Syntax highlighted by torchlight.dev --><div class='line'><span style="color: #005CC5;">Quota</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">::</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">for</span><span style="color: #24292E;">(</span><span style="color: #032F62;">&#39;api_queries&#39;</span><span style="color: #24292E;">, $user)</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">-&gt;</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">limit</span><span style="color: #24292E;">(</span><span style="color: #005CC5;">1000</span><span style="color: #24292E;">)</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">-&gt;</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">perDay</span><span style="color: #24292E;">()</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">-&gt;</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">enforce</span><span style="color: #24292E;">();</span></div></code></pre>

<h2><a id="content-quotas-on-eloquent-models" href="#content-quotas-on-eloquent-models" class="not-prose mr-1 text-gray-600 font-normal heading-permalink" aria-hidden="true" title="Permalink">#</a>Quotas on Eloquent Models</h2>
<p>Add the <code>HasQuotas</code> trait and the same builder hangs off the model:</p>
<pre><code data-theme="github-light" data-lang="php" class='torchlight' style='background-color: #fff; --theme-selection-background: #e2e5e9;'><!-- Syntax highlighted by torchlight.dev --><div class='line'><span style="color: #D73A49;">use</span><span style="color: #24292E;"> </span><span style="color: #005CC5;">ZaberDev\Quota\HasQuotas</span><span style="color: #24292E;">;</span></div><div class='line'>&nbsp;</div><div class='line'><span style="color: #D73A49;">class</span><span style="color: #24292E;"> </span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">User</span><span style="color: #24292E;"> </span><span style="color: #D73A49;">extends</span><span style="color: #24292E;"> </span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">Authenticatable</span></div><div class='line'><span style="color: #24292E;">{</span></div><div class='line'><span style="color: #24292E;">    </span><span style="color: #D73A49;">use</span><span style="color: #24292E;"> </span><span style="color: #005CC5;">HasQuotas</span><span style="color: #24292E;">;</span></div><div class='line'><span style="color: #24292E;">}</span></div></code></pre>
<pre><code data-theme="github-light" data-lang="php" class='torchlight' style='background-color: #fff; --theme-selection-background: #e2e5e9;'><!-- Syntax highlighted by torchlight.dev --><div class='line'><span style="color: #24292E;">$user</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">-&gt;</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">quota</span><span style="color: #24292E;">(</span><span style="color: #032F62;">&#39;pdf_exports&#39;</span><span style="color: #24292E;">)</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">-&gt;</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">limit</span><span style="color: #24292E;">(</span><span style="color: #005CC5;">25</span><span style="color: #24292E;">)</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">-&gt;</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">perMonth</span><span style="color: #24292E;">()</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">-&gt;</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">consume</span><span style="color: #24292E;">();</span></div><div class='line'>&nbsp;</div><div class='line'><span style="color: #24292E;">$user</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">-&gt;</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">quota</span><span style="color: #24292E;">(</span><span style="color: #032F62;">&#39;pdf_exports&#39;</span><span style="color: #24292E;">)</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">-&gt;</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">limit</span><span style="color: #24292E;">(</span><span style="color: #005CC5;">25</span><span style="color: #24292E;">)</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">-&gt;</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">perMonth</span><span style="color: #24292E;">()</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">-&gt;</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">remaining</span><span style="color: #24292E;">();</span></div></code></pre>
<p>On the database backend the records are polymorphic, so you can query a model's quotas like any other relation:</p>
<pre><code data-theme="github-light" data-lang="php" class='torchlight' style='background-color: #fff; --theme-selection-background: #e2e5e9;'><!-- Syntax highlighted by torchlight.dev --><div class='line'><span style="color: #24292E;">$activeQuotas </span><span style="color: #D73A49;">=</span><span style="color: #24292E;"> $user</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">-&gt;</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">quotas</span><span style="color: #24292E;">()</span></div><div class='line'><span style="color: #24292E;">    </span><span style="color: #D73A49;">-&gt;</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">where</span><span style="color: #24292E;">(</span><span style="color: #032F62;">&#39;period_end&#39;</span><span style="color: #24292E;">, </span><span style="color: #032F62;">&#39;&gt;&#39;</span><span style="color: #24292E;">, </span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">now</span><span style="color: #24292E;">())</span></div><div class='line'><span style="color: #24292E;">    </span><span style="color: #D73A49;">-&gt;</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">get</span><span style="color: #24292E;">();</span></div></code></pre>
<h2><a id="content-route-middleware" href="#content-route-middleware" class="not-prose mr-1 text-gray-600 font-normal heading-permalink" aria-hidden="true" title="Permalink">#</a>Route Middleware</h2>
<p>The <code>quota</code> middleware takes a name, a limit, a period, and an optional driver:</p>
<pre><code data-theme="github-light" data-lang="php" class='torchlight' style='background-color: #fff; --theme-selection-background: #e2e5e9;'><!-- Syntax highlighted by torchlight.dev --><div class='line'><span style="color: #005CC5;">Route</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">::</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">post</span><span style="color: #24292E;">(</span><span style="color: #032F62;">&#39;/exports/generate&#39;</span><span style="color: #24292E;">, [</span><span style="color: #005CC5;">ExportController</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">::class</span><span style="color: #24292E;">, </span><span style="color: #032F62;">&#39;store&#39;</span><span style="color: #24292E;">])</span></div><div class='line'><span style="color: #24292E;">    </span><span style="color: #D73A49;">-&gt;</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">middleware</span><span style="color: #24292E;">(</span><span style="color: #032F62;">&#39;quota:exports,50,month&#39;</span><span style="color: #24292E;">);</span></div><div class='line'>&nbsp;</div><div class='line'><span style="color: #005CC5;">Route</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">::</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">post</span><span style="color: #24292E;">(</span><span style="color: #032F62;">&#39;/api/v1/query&#39;</span><span style="color: #24292E;">, [</span><span style="color: #005CC5;">ApiController</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">::class</span><span style="color: #24292E;">, </span><span style="color: #032F62;">&#39;query&#39;</span><span style="color: #24292E;">])</span></div><div class='line'><span style="color: #24292E;">    </span><span style="color: #D73A49;">-&gt;</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">middleware</span><span style="color: #24292E;">(</span><span style="color: #032F62;">&#39;quota:api_query,1000,day,database&#39;</span><span style="color: #24292E;">);</span></div></code></pre>
<p>The ordering is worth noting: capacity is checked before the route runs, but the quota is only consumed when the response comes back 2xx or 3xx. A request that 500s or fails validation doesn't cost the user anything.</p>
<h2><a id="content-storage-backends" href="#content-storage-backends" class="not-prose mr-1 text-gray-600 font-normal heading-permalink" aria-hidden="true" title="Permalink">#</a>Storage Backends</h2>
<p>The default driver is <code>cache</code>, backed by whatever store you've configured — Redis and Memcached both work. The <code>database</code> driver writes to a <code>quotas</code> table instead, which is what you want when the count is tied to billing and can't evaporate with a cache flush. You can pick per call:</p>
<pre><code data-theme="github-light" data-lang="php" class='torchlight' style='background-color: #fff; --theme-selection-background: #e2e5e9;'><!-- Syntax highlighted by torchlight.dev --><div class='line'><span style="color: #005CC5;">Quota</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">::</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">for</span><span style="color: #24292E;">(</span><span style="color: #032F62;">&#39;api_ping&#39;</span><span style="color: #24292E;">, $ip)</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">-&gt;</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">using</span><span style="color: #24292E;">(</span><span style="color: #032F62;">&#39;cache&#39;</span><span style="color: #24292E;">)</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">-&gt;</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">limit</span><span style="color: #24292E;">(</span><span style="color: #005CC5;">5000</span><span style="color: #24292E;">)</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">-&gt;</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">perDay</span><span style="color: #24292E;">()</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">-&gt;</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">consume</span><span style="color: #24292E;">();</span></div><div class='line'>&nbsp;</div><div class='line'><span style="color: #005CC5;">Quota</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">::</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">for</span><span style="color: #24292E;">(</span><span style="color: #032F62;">&#39;monthly_exports&#39;</span><span style="color: #24292E;">, $user)</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">-&gt;</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">using</span><span style="color: #24292E;">(</span><span style="color: #032F62;">&#39;database&#39;</span><span style="color: #24292E;">)</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">-&gt;</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">limit</span><span style="color: #24292E;">(</span><span style="color: #005CC5;">50</span><span style="color: #24292E;">)</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">-&gt;</span><span #24292E;">();</span></div></code></pre>
<p><code>Quota::extend()</code> registers a custom backend from a service provider if you need somewhere else to put the counters, and expired database rows can be cleaned up on a schedule:</p>
<pre><code data-theme="github-light" data-lang="php" class='torchlight' style='background-color: #fff; --theme-selection-background: #e2e5e9;'><!-- Syntax highlighted by torchlight.dev --><div class='line'><span style="color: #D73A49;">use</span><span style="color: #24292E;"> </span><span style="color: #005CC5;">ZaberDev\Quota\Models\Quota</span><span style="color: #24292E;">;</span></div><div class='line'>&nbsp;</div><div class='line'><span style="color: #005CC5;">Schedule</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">::</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">command</span><span style="color: #24292E;">(</span><span style="color: #032F62;">&#39;model:prune&#39;</span><span style="color: #24292E;">, [</span><span style="color: #032F62;">&#39;--model&#39;</span><span style="color: #24292E;"> </span><span style="color: #D73A49;">=&gt;</span><span style="color: #24292E;"> </span><span style="color: #005CC5;">Quota</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">::class</span><span style="color: #24292E;">])</span><span style="color: #D73A49;">-&gt;</span><span style="color: #6F42C1;">daily</span><span style="color: #24292E;">();</span></div></code></pre>
<h2><a id="content-installation" href="#content-installation" class="not-prose mr-1 text-gray-600 font-normal heading-permalink" aria-hidden="true" title="Permalink">#</a>Installation</h2>
<p>The package requires PHP 8.2 and supports Laravel 11, 12, and 13:</p>
<pre><code data-theme="github-light" data-lang="bash" class='torchlight' style='background-color: #fff; --theme-selection-background: #e2e5e9;'><!-- Syntax highlighted by torchlight.dev --><div class='line'><span style="color: #6F42C1;">composer</span><span style="color: #24292E;"> </span><span style="color: #032F62;">require</span><span style="color: #24292E;"> </span><span style="color: #032F62;">zaber-dev/laravel-quota</span></div><div class='line'>&nbsp;</div><div class='line'><span style="color: #6F42C1;">php</span><span style="color: #24292E;"> </span><span style="color: #032F62;">artisan</span><span style="color: #24292E;"> </span><span style="color: #032F62;">vendor:publish</span><span style="color: #24292E;"> </span><span style="color: #005CC5;">--provider=</span><span style="color: #032F62;">&quot;ZaberDev\Quota\QuotaServiceProvider&quot;</span></div><div class='line'><span style="color: #6F42C1;">php</span><span style="color: #24292E;"> </span><span style="color: #032F62;">artisan</span><span style="color: #24292E;"> </span><span style="color: #032F62;">migrate</span></div></code></pre>
<p>The <code>config/quotas.php</code> configuration file sets the default driver (<code>QUOTA_DRIVER</code>), the cache store and key prefix, the database table name, and whether events are dispatched.</p>
<p>You can find installation instructions and full documentation on <a href="https://github.com/zaber-dev/laravel-quota">GitHub</a>.</p>
