<h1>Mourning Dan Williams</h1>
<div class="Byline">[Posted July 23, 2026 by corbet]
               <p>
               </div>
</div>
<div class="ArticleText"><main>
<a href="/Articles/1072861/"><img
src="https://static.lwn.net/images/conf/2026/lsfmm/DanWilliams-sm.png" alt="[Dan Williams in May 2026]"
title="Dan Williams" class="lthumb"></a>

I have just received the shocking news that Dan Williams, a longtime,
high-profile kernel developer, has passed away.  I knew him primarily
through his long service on the Linux Foundation Technical Advisory Board;
he was always a strong, thoughtful, and intelligent presence.  Dan will be
deeply missed.
<p>
There is <a href="https://www.mealtrain.com/trains/mekrzl">a support
effort</a> underway for Dan's family as they come to terms with this loss.
