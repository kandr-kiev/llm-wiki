        <h3>How this map is built</h3>
        <p>Castlemap is built entirely from open data: facts, founding dates and coordinates from <a href="https://www.wikidata.org" rel="noopener">Wikidata</a>, photographs from <a href="https://commons.wikimedia.org" rel="noopener">Wikimedia Commons</a>, stories from Wikipedia, and the night basemap from OpenFreeMap and Natural Earth. Rather than plotting every fortification on Earth, the atlas curates the 2,435 most significant castles, fortresses and palaces ГўВЂВ” each verified to have a real photo, an English Wikipedia article and exact coordinates ГўВЂВ” balanced across 129 countries so the map stays explorable.</p>
        <p>Missing a castle you know? The map refreshes from Wikidata, so the best way to add one is to improve its <a href="https://www.wikidata.org" rel="noopener">Wikidata entry</a>: exact coordinates, a Commons photo and an English Wikipedia article make it eligible for the next refresh. Edits to the commons improve every atlas built on them ГўВЂВ” not just this one.</p>
