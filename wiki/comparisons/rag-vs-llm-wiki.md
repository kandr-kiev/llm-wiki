# RAG vs LLM Wiki

| Dimension | Query-time RAG | LLM Wiki |
|---|---|---|
| Knowledge lifecycle | Retrieved per question | Compiled and maintained over time |
| Synthesis | Re-derived repeatedly | Stored in durable pages |
| Contradictions | Usually found ad hoc | Can be flagged and tracked |
| Cross-links | Often absent | Built into the wiki graph |
| Human role | Upload/query | Curate sources and steer analysis |
| Agent role | Retrieve chunks and answer | Maintain knowledge base |

## Verdict

RAG is useful for broad retrieval over large corpora. LLM Wiki is better when knowledge should compound: research programs, business-process automation, project memory, and long-running investigations.
