# Atropos

## Overview

**Atropos** — distributed reinforcement learning framework від Nous Research, використовуваний для training Hermes моделей та Hermes Agent.

**GitHub:** https://github.com/NousResearch/atropos

## Purpose

Manages asynchronous coordination across potentially **thousands of distributed workers**, addressing the challenge of highly variable LLM generation times.

## Key Features

### Rollout Handler

- Not standard RLHF — specialized rollout coordination
- Handles async distribution across workers
- Manages variable generation times (LLMs generate at different speeds)

### Rejection Sampling

In Hermes 4 training:
- Drives rejection sampling across **~1,000 task-specific verifiers**
- Filters for high-quality reasoning trajectories
- Each verifier checks different aspects of reasoning quality

### Integration with Hermes Training

Atropos integrates with:
- **DataForge** — synthetic data generation pipeline
- **GRPO** — Group Relative Policy Optimization
- **LoRA adapters** — efficient fine-tuning

## Training Scale

| Model | Samples | Tokens | Atropos Role |
|-------|---------|--------|--------------|
| Hermes 3 | 1M | 1.2B | Standard RLHF |
| Hermes 4 | ~5M | ~60B | Distributed rollout coordination |
