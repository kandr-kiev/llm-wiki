# Nous Research

## Overview

**Nous Research** — AI research laboratory, creator of:
- **Hermes** model family (3, 4, 4.3)
- **Nomos** model (Putnam math exam)
- **Psyche** decentralized compute network
- **Hermes Agent** framework (open-source, MIT license)

## Training Pipeline

### DataForge (Hermes 4)

**Graph-based synthetic data generation:**
- Each node in DAG performs struct-to-struct transformation
- Example: Wikipedia article → rap song → Q&A pair
- LLM judge evaluates: coherence, relevance, complexity, style, tone
- Iterates until sample passes or hits max retry count

**Scale comparison:**

| Metric | Hermes 3 | Hermes 4 | Growth |
|--------|----------|----------|--------|
| Samples | 1M | ~5M | 5x |
| Tokens | 1.2B | ~60B | 50x |
| Reasoning-heavy | ~1M | 3.5M | 3.5x |
| Non-reasoning | ~0 | 1.6M | New |

### Atropos

- Distributed reinforcement learning framework
- Manages asynchronous coordination across thousands of workers
- Addresses variable LLM generation times
- Drives rejection sampling across ~1,000 task-specific verifiers
- Filters for high-quality reasoning trajectories
