# DeepSeek

## Overview

DeepSeek (深度求索) is a Chinese artificial intelligence company founded in January 2023 by Liang Wenfeng, a quantitative trader at High-Flyer (幻方量化). The company has rapidly become one of the most influential AI labs globally, consistently delivering frontier-class models at a fraction of the cost of US competitors. DeepSeek is known for its architectural innovations — MoE with deep sparse routing, multi-head latent attention (MLA), and multi-round preference optimization (MPO).
