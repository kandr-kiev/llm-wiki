---# HP Inc. × OpenAI — Frontier Strategic Partnership

## Overview

HP Inc. and OpenAI have announced a strategic partnership to integrate OpenAI's frontier AI models across HP's entire device ecosystem, including consumer laptops, enterprise solutions, and printing systems.

## Key Points

- **Device integration**: OpenAI models integrated into HP laptops, workstations, and enterprise devices
- **Enterprise solutions**: AI-powered features for business workflows, document processing, and collaboration
- **Printing AI**: AI-enhanced printing and document management capabilities
- **Edge AI**: On-device AI inference capabilities powered by OpenAI's optimized models
- **Developer ecosystem**: Tools and APIs for HP developers to build AI-native applications

## Significance

This partnership represents a significant step in the integration of frontier AI models into consumer and enterprise hardware. It demonstrates the growing trend of AI-first device design and the importance of hardware-software integration in the AI era.
