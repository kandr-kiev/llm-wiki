# Kimi

## Overview

Kimi is the flagship product of Moonshot AI (月之暗面), a Chinese AI company founded in 2023 by Yang Zhilin (杨植麟), a former Tsinghua University professor and Google Brain researcher. Kimi is both a conversational AI assistant (web and app) and an open-source model series. Moonshot AI is known for pushing the boundaries of long-context understanding and agentic capabilities, with the Kimi K2 series being among the most capable open-source models globally.
