---# Mistral AI

## Overview

Mistral AI is a French artificial intelligence company founded in 2023 by former Meta AI researchers. The company has rapidly become one of the leading open-weight LLM developers, competing directly with Meta's Llama and OpenAI's GPT series. Mistral is known for its efficient models that achieve high performance with fewer parameters, strong multilingual capabilities, and commitment to European AI sovereignty.

## Infrastructure

- **Training hardware**: NVIDIA H100 clusters, European data centers
- **Compute partnerships**: Microsoft Azure, AWS, Google Cloud
- **Context window**: Up to 128K tokens (Mistral Large 2)
- **Fine-tuning**: Supports LoRA, QLoRA fine-tuning
- **Safety**: Built-in content moderation

## Business Model

Mistral operates a hybrid open-weight + commercial model:
- **Open-weight**: Mistral models are freely available for commercial use
- **API access**: Mistral API for cloud-based model access
- **Enterprise**: Custom deployments with data privacy guarantees
- **European AI sovereignty**: Positioned as European alternative to US companies
- **Revenue growth**: Rapidly growing from €0 to €100M+ ARR (2024)

## Open Source Policy

Mistral is **strongly committed to open-weight**:
- **Llama-style licensing**: Free for commercial use with restrictions
- **All models open-weight**: Weights publicly available
- **European focus**: Positioned as European alternative to US closed models
- **Community ecosystem**: Strong community support (Ollama, llama.cpp integration)
- **Competes with**: Llama (Meta), GPT (OpenAI), Claude (Anthropic)
