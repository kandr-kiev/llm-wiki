# OpenAI & Broadcom LLM-Optimized Inference Chip

## Overview

OpenAI and Broadcom announced a partnership to develop custom AI inference chips specifically optimized for large language model serving. The goal is to reduce inference latency and cost while maintaining or improving throughput.

## Key Specifications

- **Custom silicon** designed for Transformer inference workloads
- **Optimized for KV-cache** — the dominant memory bottleneck in LLM serving
- **Sparsity support** — efficient execution of MoE models
- **Interconnect** — high-bandwidth chip-to-chip communication for multi-GPU clusters

## Strategic Implications

- **Vertical integration** — OpenAI controls more of the inference stack
- **Cost reduction** — Custom chips could significantly reduce per-token inference costs
- **Competitive moat** — Hardware advantage difficult for competitors to replicate
- **Broader ecosystem** — May influence industry standards for AI inference hardware
