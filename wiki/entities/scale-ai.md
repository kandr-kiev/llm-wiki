# Scale AI

## Overview

Scale AI is an American artificial intelligence data infrastructure company founded in 2016 by Alexandr Wang and Sasha Zou. The company provides data labeling, annotation, and training data services for AI model development. Scale AI has become a critical infrastructure provider in the AI supply chain, enabling companies like OpenAI, Anthropic, and Waymo to train their models with high-quality labeled data. The company is known for its human-in-the-loop approach, quality assurance processes, and expanding product portfolio beyond data labeling.

## Models & Products

### Data Labeling Platform
- **Scale Studio** — Web-based data labeling and annotation platform
- **Scale Spark** — Automated data labeling with AI assistance
- **Scale Prompt** — Prompt engineering and evaluation platform
- **Scale Artisan** — Human expert data labeling
- **Scale Ground Truth** — High-quality labeled datasets

### AI Products
- **Scale AI API** — Data labeling and annotation API
- **Scale Artisan** — Human expert labeling for complex tasks
- **Scale Prompt** — Prompt evaluation and optimization
- **Scale Data** — Pre-built training datasets
- **Scale Model Evaluation** — Model performance benchmarking
- **Scale RLHF** — Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback services
- **Scale RAG** — Retrieval-augmented generation data services

### Specialized Data
- **Autonomous driving data** — LiDAR, camera, sensor data for self-driving cars
- **Medical imaging data** — Healthcare AI training data
- **Retail data** — Product recognition, inventory management
- **Geospatial data** — Satellite imagery, mapping data
- **Language data** — Multilingual text and speech data

## Infrastructure

- **Training hardware**: Scale's own compute infrastructure for AI-assisted labeling
- **Human workforce**: 5,000+ employees, 100,000+ contractor labelers
- **Quality assurance**: Multi-stage review process, expert verification
- **Security**: SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA compliance
- **Global**: Labeling operations in 50+ countries

## Business Model

Scale AI operates a B2B service model:
- **Data labeling services**: Pay-per-task pricing
- **Platform subscription**: Studio subscription for teams
- **Enterprise**: Custom data solutions for large organizations
- **API access**: Programmatic data labeling via API
- **Competitive strategy**: Quality and scale in data labeling

## Open Source Policy

Scale AI is **primarily a service company**:
- **No open-weight models**: Focuses on data services, not models
- **Open research**: Publishes some research papers
- **Open datasets**: Releases some labeled datasets publicly
- **Competes with**: Amazon Mechanical Turk, Appen, Labelbox
- **Philosophy**: "High-quality data is the foundation of AI progress"
