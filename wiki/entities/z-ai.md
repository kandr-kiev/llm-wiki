# Z.ai

## Overview

Z.ai (formerly Zhipu AI / 智谱AI) is a Beijing-based Chinese AI company founded in 2021 by professors and researchers from Tsinghua University's KEG Lab. The company is one of China's leading independent LLM providers, known for the GLM series of models. Zhipu went public on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange and is listed under the name "Knowledge Atlas Technology." Z.ai operates both the Chinese platform (zhipuai.cn) and the international platform (z.ai).
