I Built 4 AI Products in 6 Weeks From a Small Island in the Caribbean — Here's Everything
Tags: #showdev #buildinpublic #startup #ai #webdev
Let me paint the picture for you.
Trinidad and Tobago. Population 1.4 million. A twin-island in the southern Caribbean most people know for Carnival, steelpan, and the best doubles you'll ever eat in your life.
Not exactly Silicon Valley.
No startup ecosystem. No angel investors. No accelerators. No co-working spaces filled with hoodie-wearing founders pitching decks to VCs.
Just me. A laptop. A phone. And Claude AI.
Six weeks ago I decided to stop waiting for the "right conditions" and start building anyway.
This is what happened.

So I did.
What I Built — 4 Live Products
🤖 Product 1 — ClientLoop AI
clientloop-ai-ou16.vercel.app
The insight: most local service businesses lose 40–60% of their repeat clients not because of bad service — but because nobody ever followed up.
ClientLoop AI writes that follow-up for you.
Enter your business name, pick your niche, enter a client name — and in seconds you get a personalised:
⭐ Google review request
🔁 Referral message
💬 Win-back campaign
Ready to copy and send on WhatsApp. No complicated integrations. No tech skills needed.
Plans: $29/month · $59/month · $499 lifetime
Built as a single HTML file + Anthropic API. Deployed on Vercel.
🏗 Product 2 — SiteCraft AI
sitecraft-ai-seven.vercel.app
The insight: every local business has digital gaps costing them clients and revenue — they just can't see them clearly.
SiteCraft AI diagnoses those gaps and builds a personalised 90-day transformation plan.
Fill in your business profile. The AI scans for vulnerabilities — no booking system, no WhatsApp automation, no client follow-up, no digital income. Then it maps the exact tools to fix each one, phase by phase.
Days 1–30: Fix the foundation
Days 31–60: Build the systems
Days 61–90: Stack the income
Plans: $97 · $247 · $497
Built as a single HTML file with embedded AI engine. Deployed on Vercel.
💡 Product 3 — ProvenStack AI
provenstack-ai.vercel.app
The insight: most entrepreneurs fail not because they can't execute — but because they build something nobody wants.
ProvenStack AI solves that before you write a single line of code.
It's a live intelligence dashboard showing 247 validated SaaS and digital business ideas — each one already generating between $10,000 and $500,000 per month in real revenue globally.
Every idea comes with:
📊 Real MRR potential
⚡ Difficulty rating (Easy / Medium / Hard)
🛠 Full tool stack to build it
⏱ Time to launch estimate
Plus an AI Idea Generator — enter your skills, audience, and budget, and Claude recommends the best idea specifically for you.
